TRANSPORT

How to arrive to Verona

From Verona airport

Valerio Catullo Airport

The shuttle service from Verona Airport to Verona Porta Nuova train station operates daily at 05:35, 06:30, then every 20 minutes until 23:30

The shuttle service from Verona Porta Nuova train station to Verona Airport operates daily, at 05:15, 06:10, then every 20 minutes until 23:30

The journey takes 15 minutes.

A single ride ticket costs 6,00€. The ticket lasts 75 minutes after the punching. Travellers may use the remaining time on city buses. The unused ticket is not refundable

Aerobus information

From Bologna airport

Bologna Guglielmo Marconi Airport

The best way to arrive to Verona from Bologna airport is via Bologna Centrale train station.

The shuttle service from Bologna Airport to Bologna Centrale train station operates daily. The first trip from the airport is at 5:30 am, and the last trip is at 12:15 am. From 7:00 am to 9:30 pm a bus runs between the airport and the railway station every 11 minutes.

One-way ticket from/to the Airport 6,00€. Tickets can be purchased directly on the Aerobus website, from the automatic vending machines located in the airport, and in Bologna at the Central Railway Station (at the Aerobus departure point) or at the TPER ticket offices.

If you plan to use also the Bologna urban public transport service within the 75 minutes of validity following the first validation of your ticket, you will need to exchange your electronic ticket for a standard magnetic one. You may do this at the automatic vending machines located in the airport and at the Central Railway Station.

Aerobus information

Once you arrive to the Bologna Centrale train station, you can take a direct train to Verona Porta Nuova. A single train ticket costs between 10,00€ and 15,00€. Tickets can be purchased directly on the Trenitalia website.

Trenitalia information

From Milano-Bergamo airport

Orio al Serio Airport

The bus service from Orio al Serio Airport to Verona Porta Nuova train station operates daily by the company Flixbus.

The journey takes about 1 hour and 25 minutes.

A single ride ticket costs between 9,99€ and 15,99€.

The Venue

Villa Ca' Vendri

Villa Ca' Vendri

Contrada Vendri, 1 - 37142 Verona (Italy)

The sixteenth century Ca' Vendri is a typical Venetian villa, immersed in a silent secular 7 hectare park with a magnificent path lined with cypresses, statues, fountains and a nymphaeum colonnade.

The villa is situated at 6 kilometers from the centre of Verona, in Contrada Vendri (the Vendri quarter), a name that seems to have derived from Venus, as, in Roman times, it is said that there was a Temple there devoted to this goddess. For centuries this Villa and its park have been the ideal place for festivals, meetings and weddings.
